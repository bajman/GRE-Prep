GRE Quantitative Reasoning: Problem-Solving Approach

Three Basic Steps:

  1. Understand the Problem: Carefully read and comprehend all given information.
  2. Carry Out a Strategy: Develop a plan. Be flexible.
  3. Check Your Answer: Ensure it's reasonable and computationally correct.

Key Problem-Solving Strategies:

  • Translate Words to Math
  • Draw Figures/Diagrams
  • Graphical Representation
  • Translate Figures to Math
  • Simplify Expressions/Equations
  • Add to Geometric Figures
  • Find a Pattern
  • Search for Mathematical Relationships
  • Estimate
  • Trial and Error
  • Divide into Cases
  • Adapt Solutions
  • Check Logical Conclusions
  • Identify Missing Information
